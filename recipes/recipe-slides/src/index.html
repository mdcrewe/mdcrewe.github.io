Couscous

This recipe is for a Mediterranean couscous. I would highly recommend combining this with the chicken souvlaki recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Couscous
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 4 TBS Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 TSP of salt, 4 TBS of olive oil, and 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Once boiled, add 2 cups of couscous, turn off/reduce heat and stir.
  3. Place lid on pot for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  4. In a bowl, whisk salt, pepper, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  5. Add sauce to couscous and mix.
  6. Add optional ingredients and mix.

Chicken Souvlaki

This recipe is for a Mediterranean chicken souvlaki which we first tried when we visited Greece.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces Chicken Breast
  • 1 clove Garlic (optional)
  • 5-10 cracks Salt
  • 5-10 cracks Pepper
  • 2 TSP Oregano Flakes
  • 6 TSP Lemon Juice
  • 4 TBS Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients besides the chicken in a bowl.
  2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Swirl chicken pieces in marinade and place in a plastic bag.
  4. Leave chicken in marinade for ~20-25 minutes.
  5. Cook chicken at medium temperature until fully cooked.

Homemade Pizza

This is a quick and easy recipe for non-rising dough homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 1 TSP Sugar
  • 1 TBS Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Water

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for 5 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet.
  4. Add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake at 450°F (232°C) for 15-20 minutes.
