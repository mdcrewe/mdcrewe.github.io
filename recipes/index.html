What is this Website?

This website is just a culmination of a bunch of recipes that I enjoy making

and I wanted them all in one place so it was easy for me to access, plus it

gave me a good excuse to try and learn some HTML stuff. Also, the websites

where I found a lot of these recipes are riddled with adds and it makes them

hard to read, so here we are! All of the sections below describe the

different recipes including their required ingredients and instructions
