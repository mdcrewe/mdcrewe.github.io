Crewe Family Christmas Annual Guess The BS


What is guess the BS???

Guess the BS (or guess the blood sugar) is an annual Christmas tradition
where our extended family all guess what my Grandfather, Donald Crewe's blood
sugar reading will be on Boxing Day morning (after spending all Christmas
day eating to his heart's content). Donald, you see, is diabetic, so this
reading can widely vary and has over the years, and no matter how much logic
or medical training is used, no one really has an advantage, not even Donald,
who has yet to win in the 9 iterations of the tradition!

What are the rules?

To keep it simple, we play by The Price Is Right Rules! That simply means that
in order to win, you have to be equal to or below the final reading. If you're
0.1 above the actual reading, too bad! This does result in some strategy about
guessing after you know other people's guesses, but it is up to the participant
to determine how slimy they're willing to be! Because of this rule, we've also
had years where no one has won because everyone has guessed above the final
reading, which might make guessing low worth the thought!
