Crewe Family Christmas Annual Guess the BS

What is guess the BS???

Guess the BS (or guess the blood sugar) is an annual Christmas tradition
where our extended family all guess what my Grandfather, Donald Crewe's blood
sugar reading will be on boxing day morning (after spending all Christmas
day eating to his heart's content). Donald, you see, is diabetic, so this
reading can widely vary and has over the years, and no matter how much logic
or medical training is used, no one really has an advantage, not even Donald,
who has yet to win in the 8 iterations of the tradition!
