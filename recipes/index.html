Couscous

This recipe is for a mediteranean couscous recipe. I would highly recommend

combining this with the chicken souvlaki recipe

Pot Ingredients
Ingredient Amount
Couscous 2 Cups
Water 2 Cups
Salt 1 TSP
Olive Oil 4 TBS


Sauce Ingredients
Ingredients Amount
Salt 4 cracks
Pepper 4 cracks
Olive Oil 4-6 TBS
Lemon Juice 2-4 TBS


Optional Ingredients
Ingredients Amount
Orange Pepper ~1 Cup
Cucumber ~1 Cup
Basil ~1/2 Cup
Spinach ~1/2 Cup
Red Onion ~1/2 Cup

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 TSP of salt, 4 TBS of olive oil, and 2 cups of water in a pot
  2. Once boiled, add 2 cups of couscous, turn off/reduce heat and stir
  3. Place lid on pot for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork
  4. In a bowl, whisk 4 cracks of salt, 4 cracks of pepper, 4-6 TBS of olive oil, and 2-4 TBS of lemon juice
  5. Once couscous has been fluffed with a fork, add sauce and mix
  6. Add available optional ingredients and mix
